## 9. MAINTENANCE & PREVENTION RECOMMENDATIONS

### Transport & Storage Tips

- **Transport:**
  - Use padded board bag
  - Secure board properly to prevent movement
  - Avoid stacking heavy objects on board
  - Remove footstraps during transport to reduce stress

- **Storage:**
  - Store in cool, dry place
  - Avoid direct sunlight exposure
  - Store horizontally on padded racks
  - Remove mast and fin during long-term storage

### UV Protection

- Apply UV-stable coating annually
- Avoid prolonged sun exposure when not in use
- Use board cover when on beach
- Inspect for UV degradation regularly

### Impact Prevention

- Avoid hitting rocks, docks, or hard objects
- Use appropriate fin for conditions
- Be cautious in shallow water
- Inspect board after each session for new damage

### Regular Inspection Points

- Check mast track alignment monthly
- Inspect fin box for cracks before each session
- Check footstrap inserts for looseness
- Look for new cracks or delamination
- Test for soft spots by pressing on deck

### Early Warning Signs to Watch

- **Soft spots:** Indicates delamination or core damage
- **Cracks:** Can lead to water ingress
- **Discoloration:** May indicate water saturation
- **Changes in sound:** Hollow sounds suggest delamination
- **Visible repairs:** Monitor previous repair areas
